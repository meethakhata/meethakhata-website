Sawaal jawab

Aapke mann mein kya sawaal hain?

Kya ye app mobile pe chalega?+
Haan bilkul! Android phone, iPhone, aur tablet — sab pe. Seedha meethakhata.in kholo Chrome mein aur "Add to Home Screen" karo. Bilkul native app jaisa feel aayega.
Internet na ho toh kya hoga?+
Offline billing bhi kaam karta hai! Bills bante rahe. Jab internet aayega, automatically sab cloud pe sync ho jaayega. Counter pe internet ki tension nahi.
Mera data safe rahega?+
Bilkul safe hai. Aapka data Indian servers pe store hota hai (Mumbai region). Bank jaisi encryption hai. Sirf aap dekh sakte hain.
Staff ko training chahiye?+
10 minute mein koi bhi counter staff billing seekh jaata hai. Hum onboarding visit karte hain aur Hindi video guide bhi milti hai.
Kitne counters chal sakte hain?+
Basic plan mein 1 counter, Pro mein 4 counters, Premium mein unlimited counters.
Cancel karna easy hai?+
Haan, koi lock-in contract nahi hai. Kabhi bhi cancel kar sakte hain Settings se. Data 30 din tak safe, download kar sakte hain.
